I’m a senior student at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy majoring in Global Studies, minoring in Computer Science and Intelligent Environment.

My research interests lie in designing and building human-centered technologies that would inspire meaningful and creative interactions. I’m particularly interested in tangible, reconfigurable, and responsive representation that brings digital information into 3D spaces, thus freeing storytellers from material constraints and enabling them to tell stories with much stronger rhetorics.

Currently I’m a research assistant at Tsinghua University’s Pervasive Computing Group advised by Prof Chun Yu and a research intern at the Future Lab advised by Yingqing Li.
